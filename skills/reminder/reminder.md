# Set Reminders and Alarms

## Description
Set reminders and alarms for specific times or after a delay. The bot will send you a notification at the scheduled time. Reminders can be one-time or recurring.

## Usage
When a user asks to be reminded about something or wants to set an alarm, use this skill to schedule it.

Format: "Reminder: [message] at [time/date]" or "Reminder: [message] in [duration]"

Time formats supported:
- Specific time: "3:30pm", "15:30", "3pm tomorrow"
- Relative time: "in 5 minutes", "in 2 hours", "in 30 seconds"
- Specific date/time: "January 31 at 2pm", "tomorrow at 9am"

## Examples
- "Reminder: Call mom at 5pm today"
- "Reminder: Take medicine in 30 minutes"
- "Reminder: Meeting tomorrow at 10am"
- "Reminder: Workout in 2 hours"
- "Reminder: Check email at 9am Monday"
- "Reminder: Birthday party on February 14 at 6pm"
