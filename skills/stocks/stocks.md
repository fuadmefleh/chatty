# Stocks

## Description
Real-time stock market data including top gainers, top losers, most active stocks, and individual ticker lookups. Enhanced with LLM-powered analysis for daily market summaries, news sentiment correlation with price movements, and proactive watchlist alerts for significant trends.

## Usage
Use this skill when the user asks about stock performance, market movers, specific stock tickers, market sentiment, or daily market overviews.

**Basic data tools:**
- `/stocks` or "show market movers" — today's top gainers, losers, and most active stocks
- "Tell me about AAPL" — detailed ticker info (price, P/E, market cap, etc.)

**LLM-powered analysis tools:**
- "Give me today's market summary" — AI-generated daily market overview covering sentiment, standout movers, and volume highlights
- "What's the sentiment on TSLA?" — AI analysis of news sentiment correlated with price movement
- "Check my watchlist" / "Are any of AAPL, MSFT, TSLA moving?" — AI-powered alerts for stocks with significant daily moves or unusual volume

## Examples
- "Show me today's stock market movers"
- "What are the top gaining stocks today?"
- "Tell me about AAPL stock"
- "/stocks"
- "Give me a market summary for today"
- "What's the news sentiment on NVDA?"
- "Check if any of my watched stocks (AAPL, TSLA, MSFT) are moving significantly"
- "Is TSLA's price movement aligned with the news sentiment?"
