# Stocks

## Description
Real-time stock market data including top gainers, top losers, most active stocks, and individual ticker lookups.

## Usage
Use this skill when the user asks about stock performance, market movers, or specific stock tickers. The `/stocks` command shows today's top market movers. The agent can also look up individual tickers.

## Examples
- "Show me today's stock market movers"
- "What are the top gaining stocks today?"
- "Tell me about AAPL stock"
- "/stocks"
