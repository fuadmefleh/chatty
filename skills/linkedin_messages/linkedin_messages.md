# LinkedIn Messages

## Description
Reads the user's LinkedIn messages, feed, and connections through their connected LinkedIn account (linked by pasting a session cookie on the dashboard's Settings page). Read-only - never sends messages or posts on the user's behalf.

## Usage
Use this skill when the user wants to:
- See recent LinkedIn conversations or messages
- Read what was said in a specific LinkedIn conversation
- Get a summary of their LinkedIn feed
- See their LinkedIn connections/network

## Examples
- "Do I have any new LinkedIn messages?"
- "What's in my LinkedIn feed today?"
- "Show me my LinkedIn connections"
- "What did that recruiter say on LinkedIn?"
